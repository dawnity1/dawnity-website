Engineering Consulting Services

Independent engineering consulting for aerospace and semiconductor applications. Focused on depth, precision, and execution.

What Dawnity Does

Dawnity supports engineering organizations with high-complexity analysis, development, and systems engineering work. Engagements range from deep technical problem solving to cross-domain systems engineering support.

Mechanical & Structural Engineering

Structural analysis, load paths, design validation, and FEM model correlation for high-precision systems for aerospace and semicondutor applications.

Systems Engineering

Requirement analysis, system decomposition, interface definition, and technical coordination across domains.

Engineering Software Development

Development of engineering tools, analysis scripts, and automation to support design and validation workflows.

How Dawnity Works

Dawnity operates as an independent consulting partner. Work is primarily performed remotely, with on-site presence when required. Engagements are structured to integrate seamlessly with existing engineering teams while remaining contractually independent.

About

Dawnity was founded to provide focused, senior-level engineering expertise without the overhead and rigidity of large consulting organizations. The objective is simple: deliver clarity and progress on technically demanding problems.
